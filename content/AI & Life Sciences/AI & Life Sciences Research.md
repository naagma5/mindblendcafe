- Challenges in building AI systems

	- Structural barriers (culture, org, legal) 
	- Gaps in knowledge 
	- Advancing knowledge 
	- Cost of adoption 
- NEED public participation/input
- Have to educate - AI literacy
- Integrate ethical reflection in AI development
- Citizens distrust use of AI bc
	- privacy violation 
	- question if outcomes are fair 
	- accountability issues if things go wrong
	- high-risk if fails
	- competing stakeholder interest
- How to address ^ issues?
	- Public engagement / debate *works better if it's specific > ambiguous*
	- Education 
- Critical ethical guidelines
	- Finnish public
		- transparency (purpose of system, impact), privacy, data dignity (control/consent) 
	- industry & institution 
		- **Guidelines**: transparency, justice & fairness, non-maleficence (security & safety), responsibility (accountability), privacy
		- **Philosophy:** values, principles (fairness, accountability, sustainability, transparency), governance framework 
		- **Strategy**: citizen-centric, citizen input, build on existing resources, data-prepared, mitigate ethical risks, avoid AI decision making, augment employees > replace
